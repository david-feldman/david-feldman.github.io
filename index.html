David Feldman

Currently a Product Manager at Hive AI in San Francisco, I lead design and execution for Mensio, Hive's flagship media analytics platform. I've also personally developed much of the statistical methodology that powers the product.
---
Before coming to Hive I spent two years at Accenture Digital, where I primarily worked on strategy and analytics engagements at Google as a consultant. While consulting at Google, I built an analytics framework for a major AdWords user-research initiative, conducted market research to assess feature importance for the launch of a developing market file sharing app, and piloted device-usage-based email newsletters for the Google Home product suite. I hold a M.S. in Computer Science from Johns Hopkins University, and a B.S. in Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering, also from Johns Hopkins.
